Account & Data Deletion Request

At Resume Elite, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

What data will be deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

  • Your profile information (Name, Email, Profile Picture).
  • All saved resume data and custom templates.
  • Your ATS scoring history and career preferences.
  • Your saved job applications or bookmarks.

How to request deletion

To delete your account, please send an email from the email address associated with your Resume Elite account to:

Email: dineshmahi02@gmail.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request - [Your Name]

Once we receive your email, we will verify your identity and process the deletion within 7-14 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

Manual Data Deletion

If you do not want to delete your entire account, you can manually delete individual resumes or job data directly within the app settings at any time. Manually deleted items are removed instantly from your view and purged from our databases shortly after.
